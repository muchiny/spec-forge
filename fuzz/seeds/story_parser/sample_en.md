# User Stories - Library

## Search by ISBN

As a librarian, I want to search for a book by ISBN so that I can quickly locate a specific title.

- The input field accepts ISBN-10 and ISBN-13 formats
- Results display in under 2 seconds

## Online Registration

As a future member, I want to register online so that I can borrow books without visiting in person.

- The form requires name, email and address
- A confirmation email is sent automatically
